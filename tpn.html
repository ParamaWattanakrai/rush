HISTORY

Triam Udom Suksa Pattanakarn School, Nonthaburi, formerly known as Bang Yai School It was established on April 8, 1959 at Wat Pikun Ngoen, Bang Muang Subdistrict, Bang Yai District, Nonthaburi Province. By the initiative of Acting Sub-Lieutenant Sawat Duangchan, Education Subdistrict of Bang Yai District at that time open for the first time Mathayomsuksa 1 uses the Phra Pariyatidhamma Hall of Wat Pikul Ngoen as a temporary study location. Subsequently, additional classes were extended until Grade 3. In 1961, the government gave a budget to build a temporary school building with 6 classrooms, but the budget was not complete. Wat Pikul Ngoen And the people of Bang Yai District have donated another 50,000 baht in 1969. The school has joined the Rural High School Improvement Project (M.Ed. g) The 6th generation, after that, received a budget to build a canteen, assembly hall, and teacher's residence. When the number of classrooms gradually increased to 10 classrooms, the school building was insufficient and the land could not be expanded. Therefore contacted to use the land in the area of ​​Wat Bot Don Phrom, which has the royal preechamuni (Porn Pawaro), the primate of Nonthaburi province. At that time, he was a caretaker of the school, receiving good courtesy from the Abbot, the temple committee and the people.

And on September 25, 1969, the provincial primate made a letter of consent to be used at the Sangha Monastery. Wat Bot Don Phrom Let's build a school building. Bang Yai School therefore moved to teach in a new place. Wat Bot Don Prom on May 22, 1971, and in 1978, the school opened a high school level (M.E. 4) and has constantly improved and developed both in terms of administration teaching and learning management, building, place and environment to make it beautiful more pleasant And in the academic year 1992, it was selected as a school receiving the Royal Secondary School Award for the medium-sized level of the academic year 1992. In addition, the school was also selected by the Department of Education to participate in the Opportunity Expansion Program. in education at the lower secondary level and was selected for the secondary school development project in the metropolitan area around Bangkok as an extraction point to deter a number of students from being crowded in the central Later, it was approved by Khunying Boonluen, Trachu Group, and the Association of Parents and Teachers of Triam Udom Suksa School. To be a school in the affiliated school of Triam Udom Suksa School It is a sister school of Triam Udom Suksa Pattanakarn School. and added the name to Triam Udom Suksa Pattanakarn School and received royal permission
